A Cultural Tour of Ireland

Final project for Geography 572: Graphic Design in Cartography. My first semester back at UW after being abroad, I chose to create a tourism map that depicted not only some of the popular places that you hear about, but also some of the lesser known places that I personally enjoyed. Toasties after a long day of hiking are highly recommended.
