About Me

I am a senior geography student at the University of Wisconsin-Madison, which is also my hometown. A proud Midwesterner, I enjoy mapping and studying the physical processes and phenomena (both natural and otherwise) of the region and beyond. I spent a semester on the rustic west coast of Ireland in Galway, where I was also able to study geography and gain a unique perspective on the same discipline I study back home.

Personal Interests

A city kid with a great appreciation for the outdoors and countryside, I enjoy basketball, hiking, canoeing, and lake swimming. I've been a radio DJ in both Madison and Galway, and have an eclectic taste in music, reserving a special place for local artists.
