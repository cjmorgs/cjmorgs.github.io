About Me

I am a master's student in the Natural Resources and Environmental Studies program at the University of Northern British Columbia, focusing on Outdoor Recreation, Conservation, and Tourism. I have previously worked as a planner for the Minnesota Department of Transportation, and before that graduated with a BA from the University of Wisconsin-Madison. A proud Midwesterner, I enjoy mapping and studying both the physical and cultural features of the region and beyond.

Personal Interests

A city kid with a great appreciation for the outdoors and countryside, I enjoy basketball, hiking, canoeing, and lake swimming. I have a deep appreciation for parks and how they connect people to nature; serving as an intersection of recreation and wildlife habitat.

Additionally, I've been a radio DJ in both Madison and Galway, Ireland during an undergraduate semester abroad. I have a fairly eclectic taste in music, reserving a special place for local artists. I'm also a sucker for geographic references in song lyrics.
